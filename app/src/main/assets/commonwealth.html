Commonwealth Bank series:

Earlier at the beginning stage it was known as Benson and Hedges World Series.  Later Carlton and United then VB series and now it is known as CB series. It is an official Australian series held during the months of Dec and Jan-Feb. Just after the Christmas and continuing Australian summer season. This tournament has benefited the hosting team to the fullest as they won 19 out of 33 times. No other team performed gloriously apart from West Indies at the beginning stages. But it never disappointed fans and cricket lovers. And ICC always gives it a lot of importance in its Cricket Calendar.
