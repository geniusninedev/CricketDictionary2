Indian Premier League:

Who doesn’t know this mega cricket extravaganza? This is the India’s most viewed sport event. And also has its mark in other nations as well. This is the tournament which unites youngsters, Indian players and Internationals players in the same team and gives a perfect example for united we stand no matter how divided we are. Most of the matches are nerve cracking and exciting. This is the tournament which goes for 2 long months and can be treated as summer bonanza. So far there are been 6 seasons and almost all were in news in every possible way. It is also the care of address for many controversies and recent IPL 6 has taken it to the peaks. But still this cricket tournament is the combination of glitz, glory and glamour. IPL is a trim mix of Cinema and cricket and a perfect combo of Sport and politics.
