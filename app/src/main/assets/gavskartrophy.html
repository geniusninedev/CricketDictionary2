Border Gavaskar Trophy:

This is a battle of 2 nations India and Australia. The format is test and it has witnessed the most exciting test matches ever. It was named as the gratitude for Australia’s Allan Border and India’s Sunil Gavaskar as both were first to reach the 10,000 club and were the skippers of the respective teams at that time. They hailed the test cricket at that time. India has been very successful and dominant in this tournament as it won 7 titles so far.
