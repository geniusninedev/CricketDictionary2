NatWest series:

This is the exclusive England based One day International tournament that was justifyed in the year 2000 and sponsored by National Westminster Bank.  It is generally the tri angular series where each team meets another 2 times in the series and the best two teams fight for finals at The Mecca of Cricket Lords. The most historical match was the final in the year 2004 where India chased the huge target of 324 against England. And no one can forget the dada’s shirt twirling moments at the Lord’s gallery which will be reminisced  until the game exist. All the qualified test teams have played this tournament so far. And it has been the prestigious tournament for the England Cricket and challenging for the other Nations as well.
